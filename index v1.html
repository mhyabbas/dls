How it works Booking interpreters is made simple with the Digi Language Support app

Digi Language Support provides a seamless digital self-service booking system for practitioners and administrators of primary and community-based services to access telehealth interpreting services. Our goal is to enhance access and participation of non-English speaking or limited English speaking consumers to health services offered by primary and community-based services.

Request

Request the type of interpreting session you would like along with the language required and preferred booking time

Match

The Digi Language support app will match your request with suitable interpreters available at your preferred booking time and notify them of your request.

Accept

One of the matched interpreters will accept your booking request, normally within 2 hours. The app will send you a notification letting you know who has accepted your request and details about how to contact them.

Interpret

Your interpreter will carry out the services you have requested and you will have a chance to rate your experience.
